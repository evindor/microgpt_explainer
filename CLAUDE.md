We are building an explainer website for Andrej Karpathy's microgpt - a tiny, 200 lines of code transformer.
The explainer website is targeting college dropout software developers who know nothing about calculus, linear algebra, machine learning or GPTs, but are willing to learn.
The explainer also targets visdual learners - to explain complex concepts with intecatie visualizations.
In `microgpt/microgpt.py` we have the source code of microgpt.
In `microgpt/microgpt.html` we have the blog post by Andrej Karpathy explaining his creation.
Our goal is to faithfully follow the blog post, but add to the explanations with visualizations and occasional detours to explain mathematicla and ML concepts.
